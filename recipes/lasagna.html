Ultimate Low-Carb Zucchini Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 1 1/2 large zucchinis
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 container ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 parmesan cheese
  • Aluminum foil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease an 8-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
  2. Pat dry zucchini slices with a paper towel to remove excess moisture. Set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium-high heat. Add ground beef; cook until browned, 5 to 8 minutes.
  4. Add marinara sauce, 1 teaspoon salt, oregano, and pepper; simmer for 10 minutes. Set aside.
  5. Combine ricotta cheese, egg, 1 teaspoon salt, and nutmeg in a bowl; mix well. Set aside.
  6. Arrange one layer of zucchini slices in the prepared baking dish. Cover with 1/2 of the sauce. Add another layer
    of zucchini slices. Spread ricotta mixture on top. Sprinkle with 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese. Add another layer
    of zucchini slices; cover with remaining sauce and top with remaining mozzarella cheese and Parmesan cheese.
    Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil.
  7. Add marinara sauce, 1 teaspoon salt, oregano, and pepper; simmer for 10 minutes. Set aside.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Remove aluminum foil and bake until top is golden, about 15 minutes more.
